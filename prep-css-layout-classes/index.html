The Halo Series

The game takes place in a 26th century version of Earth, where the United Nations Space Command (UNSC) develops a secret program for the development of supersoldiers called the Spartans to help against the war between Earth and The Covenant.

Halo: Combat Evolved

In Halo CE, the Master Chief fights the alien species called The Covenant, who are trying to use the ringworld named Halo, believed to be a weapon, to destroy Earth. Organisms called The Flood inhabit the ring and can take over deceased bodies of any species. It is up to the Master Chief to eliminate the Flood by destroying the Halo installation.

Halo 2

In Halo 2 Covenant commander, stripped of his status and rank, is given a new role in the fight against the UNSC, called the Arbiter. After the Covenant attack an Earth base again, the UNSC discover a new Halo installation, and the Master Chief needs to destry this one as well. The Arbiter is exiled from the Covenant and the honor guard of the Prophets, the leaders of the Covenant, deploy Tartarus, leader of the Brutes, to eliminate the Arbiter and the Covenant that aided the rebellion against the Covenant.

Halo 3

The Master Chief and the Arbiter join forces to stop the Covenant from activating a Forerunner artifact that can aid in the destruction of the UNSC if not stopped. After the Covenant is stopped, the humans and Elites go through a portal and find a structure in space called the Ark, which can activate the Halo installations remotely. The Master Chief and Arbiter must stop the Ark from being activated.
